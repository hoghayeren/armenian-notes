| Person       | Eastern Armenian | Western Armenian |
| ------------ | ----------------- | ----------------- |
| I            | ես (yes)           | ես (yes)           |
| you (sing.)  | դու (du)           | դուն (tun)         |
| he/she/it    | նա (na)            | ան (an)            |
