# Personal Pronouns & "To Be"

## Lesson Objectives
1. Learn the personal pronouns I, you (singular/informal), he, she, it, we, you (plural/formal), and they in Armenian.
2. Learn how to conjugate the verb “to be” and be able to say “I am”, “She is”, “They are”, etc. in Eastern Armenian.

### Eastern Armenian Personal Pronouns

|            | Singular | Plural |
| ---------- | --------------| ---------|
| 1st Person | Ես (I) | Մենք (We)|
| ---------- | --------------| ---------|
| 2nd Person | Դու (You) | Դուք (You)|
|            |Informal, Singular | Formal, Plural |
| ---------- | --------------| ---------|
| 3rd Person | Նա (He/She/It) | Նրանք (They) |
