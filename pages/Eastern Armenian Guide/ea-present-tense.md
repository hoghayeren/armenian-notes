# **Present Tense**

## **Lesson Objectives**
* Learn about Eastern Armenian verb infinitives.
* Learn how to conjugate verbs in the present tense.

## **Eastern Armenian Verb Infinitives**
In Eastern Armenian, verb infinitives have two endings: **-ել** or **-ալ**. Below are a few examples of Armenian verb infinitives.

| Verb Infinitives | Examples |
|-----------------|--------------------|
|Խոսել 	- to speak | աշխատել - to work |
|-----------------|--------------------|
| Ուտել - to eat | անել - to do |
|-----------------|--------------------|
| Խմել - to drink | գնալ - to go |
|-----------------|--------------------|
| Կարդալ - to read | գրել - to write |
|-----------------|--------------------|

To conjugate these verbs in present tense, take out the **-ել** or **-ալ** ending and replace it with **-ում**. Then, add the auxiliary verbs after your newly constructed verb as shown below with the verb Խոսել.

|            | Singular | Plural |
| ---------- | --------------| ---------|
| 1st Person | Ես խոսում **եմ**| Մենք խոսում **ենք** |
| ---------- | --------------| ---------|
| | I am speaking or I speak | We are speaking or We speak|
| ---------- | --------------| ---------|
| 2nd Person | Դու խոսում *ես** | Դուք խոսում **եք** |
| | You (inf., sing.) are speaking or You speak | You (form., pl.) are speaking or You speak|
| ---------- | --------------| ---------|
| 3rd Person | Նա խոսում **է** | Նրանք խոսում **են**  |
| | He/She/It is speaking or He/She/It speaks | They are speaking or They speak |
| ---------- | --------------| ---------|
