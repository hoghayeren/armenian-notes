# **Armenian Alphabet**
<br>
The Armenian alphabet (Հայոց այբուբեն) was developed around 405 AD by Mesrop Mashtots (Մեսրոպ Մաշտոց), an Armenian linguist, composer, theologian, and venerated saint in the Armenian Apostolic Church and Armenian Catholic Church. Below is considered the first sentence to have been written down in the Armenian script.

> «ճանաչել զիմաստութիւն եւ զխրատ, իմանալ զբանս հանճարոյ։» <br>
> "To know wisdom and instruction; to perceive the words of understanding."
>
> \- *Book of Proverbs*, 1:2
