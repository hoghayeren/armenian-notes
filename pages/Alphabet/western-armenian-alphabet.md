## **Letters and their English Pronunciations**

{: .note-title }
> Compound Vowels
>
> ՈՒ ու is a compound vowel and is pronounced like "oo" in boot <br>
> իւ is a compound vowel and it is pronounced like "i-oo"

{: .note-title }
> \* Additional Notes to reduce spelling errors
>
> * Գ գ is more common at the beginning of words than Ք ք
> * The sound "k" at the end of a verb is always ք
> * ք is more common in noun endings than գ

<br>

{: .note-title }
> \*\* The Յ Letter
>
> * Յ յ is pronounced "h" at beginning of a word
> * it is silent if it is at the end of a word (ex. Կայ) with exceptions
>   * հայ is pronounced like "high" and թէյ is pronounced like "tey"
>   * after ա, է, ի in the middle of a word, it is pronounced like y (why) as seen in այս (pronounced like "ice")
>   * if sandwiched between ո and a consonant, then ո and յ make ոյ to make an "ooy" sound (ex. քոյր which is pronounced like "kooyr")
>   * if sandwiched between ո and a vowel, then յ is just the y (yuh) sound
