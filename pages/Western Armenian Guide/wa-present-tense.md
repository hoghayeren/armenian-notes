## **Western Armenian Verb Infinitives**
There are a few details and differences when it comes to conjugating verbs in the present tense in Western Armenian. In Eastern Armenian, verb infinitives have two endings: **-ել** or **-ալ**. These are present in Western Armenian as well, but there is an additional group of verb infinitives with the ending **-իլ**. Below are some examples:

* Խօսիլ - to speak
* ընել - to do
* ապրիլ - to live
* աշխատիլ - work
* սիրել - to love/like
* տեսնել - to see
* երթալ - to go
* կարդալ - to read

{: .note-title }
> Why is the spelling different from Eastern Armenian?
>
> In Eastern Armenian, “to speak” is spelled **Խոսել** while in Western Armenian it is **Խօսիլ**. “To do” is spelled **ընել** in Western Armenian, but in Eastern Armenian it is **անել**.
> This difference between the two dialects is because Western Armenian uses classical orthography or Mashtotsian orthography (Հայերէնի դասական ուղղագրութիւն). Most of Eastern Armenian uses reformed orthography which was a result of the Armenian orthography reform in 1920s Soviet Armenia.

## **Conjugating Verbs in Present Tense**

Now the major differences show up here when conjugating present tense in Western Armenian. This is the formula that applies when conjugating present tense:

<div style="text-align: center; font-size: 2em; font-weight: bold;">

կը + [conjugated verb]

</div>

The կը is a particle and it is placed before the conjugated verb. Remember the 3 groups of verb infinitives we covered above? All 3 of those cases are conjugated differently from each other and they are shown below.
<br>
<br>
<br>


<div markdown="1" style="border: 2px solid #e67e22; background-color: #fff3e0; padding: 10px; border-radius: 4px; display: inline-block;">

### First Group Verbs: -ել
{: style="margin: 0 !important; line-height: normal !important; font-size: 1.3em !important; font-weight: bold !important;" }

</div>

By following the formula shown above, conjugating -ել verbs happens by writing the Կը particle, then dropping the -ել ending of the verb, and replacing it with **-եմ**, **-ես**, **-է**, **-ենք**, **-էք**, or **-են** based on the person.


|            | Singular | Translation | Plural | Translation |
| ---------- | ---------| ------------| ------ | ----------- |
| 1st Person | Ես կը սիր**եմ**|I like | Մենք կը սիր**ենք** | We like|
| ---------- | --------------| ---------| --------------| ---------|
| 2nd Person | Դուն կը սիր**ես** | You (inf., sing.) like | Դուք կը սիր**էք** | You (form., pl.) like|
| ---------- | --------------| ---------| --------------| ---------|
| 3rd Person | Ան or Ինք կը սիր**է** | He/She/It likes | Անոնք or Իրենք կը սիր**են**  | They like |
| ---------- | --------------| ---------| --------------| ---------| 

<br>
<br>

<div markdown="1" style="border: 2px solid #e67e22; background-color: #fff3e0; padding: 10px; border-radius: 4px; display: inline-block;">

### Second Group Verbs: -ալ
{: style="margin: 0 !important; line-height: normal !important; font-size: 1.3em !important; font-weight: bold !important;" }

</div>

Similarly to the first group, conjugating -ալ verbs happens by writing the Կը particle, then dropping the -ալ ending of the verb, but replacing it with **-ամ**, **-աս**, **-այ**, **-անք**, **-աք**, or **-ան** based on the person.


|            | Singular | Translation | Plural | Translation |
| ---------- | ---------| ------------| ------ | ----------- |
| 1st Person | Ես կը կարդ**ամ** |I read | Մենք կը կարդ**անք** | We read|
| ---------- | --------------| ---------| --------------| ---------|
| 2nd Person | Դուն կը կարդ**աս** | You (inf., sing.) read | Դուք կը կարդ**աք**| You (form., pl.) read|
| ---------- | --------------| ---------| --------------| ---------|
| 3rd Person | Ան or Ինք կը կարդ**այ** | He/She/It reads | Անոնք or Իրենք կը կարդ**ան**  | They read |
| ---------- | --------------| ---------| --------------| ---------| 

<br>
<br>


<div markdown="1" style="border: 2px solid #e67e22; background-color: #fff3e0; padding: 10px; border-radius: 4px; display: inline-block;">

### Third Group Verbs: -իլ
{: style="margin: 0 !important; line-height: normal !important; font-size: 1.3em !important; font-weight: bold !important;" }

</div>

And finally, similarly to the other two groups, conjugating -իլ verbs happens by writing the Կը particle, then dropping the -իլ ending of the verb, but replacing it with **-իմ**, **-իս**, **-ի**, **-ինք**, **-իք**, or **-ին** based on the person.

|            | Singular | Translation | Plural | Translation |
| ---------- | ---------| ------------| ------ | ----------- |
| 1st Person | Ես կը խօս**իմ** |I speak | Մենք կը խօս**ինք** | We speak|
| ---------- | --------------| ---------| --------------| ---------|
| 2nd Person | Դուն կը խօս**իս** | You (inf., sing.) speak | Դուք կը խօս**իք**| You (form., pl.) speak|
| ---------- | --------------| ---------| --------------| ---------|
| 3rd Person | Ան or Ինք կը խօս**ի**| He/She/It speaks | Անոնք or Իրենք կը խօս**ին**  | They speak |
| ---------- | --------------| ---------| --------------| ---------| 

<br>
<br>


<div markdown="1" style="border: 2px solid #e67e22; background-color: #fff3e0; padding: 10px; border-radius: 4px; display: inline-block;">

### Special Case for Verbs Starting with a Vowel
{: style="margin: 0 !important; line-height: normal !important; font-size: 1.3em !important; font-weight: bold !important;" }

</div>

If a verb starts with a vowel such as ա, ը, ե, etc. then the ը in the particle կը gets dropped and is replaced with an abatarts (ապաթարց). The formula changes into the following:

<div style="text-align: center; font-size: 2em; font-weight: bold;">

կ՚[conjugated verb]

</div>

Below are two examples with the verbs ընել and աշխատիլ.

|            | Singular | Translation | Plural | Translation |
| ---------- | ---------| ------------| ------ | ----------- |
| 1st Person | Ես կ՚ըն**եմ** |I do | Մենք կ՚ըն**ենք**| We do |
| ---------- | --------------| ---------| --------------| ---------|
| 2nd Person | Դուն կ՚ըն**ես** | You (inf., sing.) do | Դուք կ՚ըն**եք**| You (form., pl.) do|
| ---------- | --------------| ---------| --------------| ---------|
| 3rd Person | Ան or Ինք կ՚ըն**է**| He/She/It does | Անոնք or Իրենք կ՚ըն**են** | They do |
| ---------- | --------------| ---------| --------------| ---------|
