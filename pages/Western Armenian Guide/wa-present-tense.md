# **Present Tense**

## **Lesson Objectives**
* Learn about Western Armenian verb infinitives.
* Learn how to conjugate verbs in the present tense in Western Armenian.

## **Western Armenian Verb Infinitives**
There are a few details and differences when it comes to conjugating verbs in the present tense in Western Armenian. In Eastern Armenian, verb infinitives have two endings: **-ել** or **-ալ**. These are present in Western Armenian as well, but there is an additional group of verb infinitives with the ending **-իլ**. Below are some examples:

* Խօսիլ - to speak
* ընել - to do
* ապրիլ - to live
* աշխատիլ - work
* սիրել - to love/like
* տեսնել - to see
* երթալ - to go
* կարդալ - to read

{: .note-title }
> Why is the spelling different from Eastern Armenian?
>
> In Eastern Armenian, “to speak” is spelled **Խոսել** while in Western Armenian it is **Խօսիլ**. “To do” is spelled **ընել** in Western Armenian, but in Eastern Armenian it is **անել**.
> This difference between the two dialects is because Western Armenian uses classical orthography or Mashtotsian orthography (Հայերէնի դասական ուղղագրութիւն). Most of Eastern Armenian uses reformed orthography which was a result of the Armenian orthography reform in 1920s Soviet Armenia.

## **Conjugating Verbs in Present Tense**

Now the major differences show up here when conjugating present tense in Western Armenian. This is the formula that applies when conjugating present tense:

<div style="text-align: center; font-size: 2em; font-weight: bold;">

կը + [conjugated verb]

</div>

The կը is a particle and it is placed before the conjugated verb. Remember the 3 groups of verb infinitives we covered above? All 3 of those cases are conjugated differently from each other and they are shown below.

By following the formula shown above, conjugating -ել verbs happens by writing the Կը particle, then dropping the -ել ending of the verb, and replacing it with **-եմ**, **-ես**, **-է**, **-ենք**, **-էք**, or **-են** based on the person.
